Welcome to Desserlicious

Our story

We create sinfully delicious healthy snacks to help satisfy your sugar cravings with healthy alternatives. Your health is of extreme importance to us, so is your right to enjoy decandent snacks. This dessert business was started in 1955 and is now run by the 3rd generation of the original founder. Desserlicious was started out by Mrs Maria Onyeneke from of a desire to curb obesity in her 1st child, Chidi; she was a renowned baker with Unilever and put her skills in use by swapping certain unhealthy ingredients for healthier alternatives. We still use the same amazing receipe and have been serving Nigerians for 3 generations now. What are you waiting for? Give us a try today.
