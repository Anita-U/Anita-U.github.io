Desserlicious

Born out of a passion to raise healthy kids, our mission for 3 generations has been the same; to provide
safe and healthy natural snacks to Nigerians and eliminate obesity.

We are a 3rd generation of healthy and delicious snacks bakers and our secret recipes have been guarded jealously while
being past down from generations since the 1950s. We are currently the largest healthy dessert company in West Africa catering to thousands of people who want to live long and healthy lives.

All desserts are freshly made in our state-of-the-art factories with natural ingredients; this means, no artificial flavours, colours, sweetners or preservatives.
We are your must trusted healthy, natural dessert brand.

We care about you and about the environment, this is why all our packaging are a 100% bio-degradable and recyclable.
We also purchase our ingredients from reputable brands that are eco-conscious and eco-friendly.

Take a tour through our menu today and indulge yourself in absolutely delicious and health friendly treats created with you and your kin in mind.
